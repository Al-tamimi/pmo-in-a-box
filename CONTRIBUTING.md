# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving **PMO-in-a-Box**! This is a community resource
for anyone building or maturing a Project Management Office. Contributions of all
sizes are welcome: from fixing a typo to adding a whole new template.

## Ways to contribute
- **Improve a template**: clearer wording, better structure, more useful guidance.
- **Add a template**: something genuinely useful that fills a gap.
- **Translate**: the toolkit is currently English-only; clean translations are welcome.
- **Share feedback**: open an issue describing what helped or what was confusing.

## Ground rules (please read before opening a PR)
To keep this toolkit safe to reuse by everyone, every contribution **must**:

1. **Be brand-neutral.** No company logos, no trademarks, no real organization names.
   Use the standard placeholders instead:
   `[Your Organization]`, `[Your Country]`, `[Your Regulator]`, `[Partner Organization]`,
   `[City]`, `[Name]`.
2. **Contain no confidential or personal data.** No real project data, financials,
   client names, or staff names: in the document body **or** its metadata
   (author/company fields).
3. **Respect copyright.** Only contribute material you have the right to share under
   [CC BY 4.0](LICENSE). Do not paste in vendor courseware or copyrighted manuals.
4. **Be in English** and use clear, plain language.

## How to submit
1. Fork the repository and create a branch (`feature/short-description`).
2. Make your change. For Office files, double-check **File ▸ Properties** to confirm
   no personal name or company is left in the metadata.
3. Open a Pull Request describing **what** you changed and **why**.

By contributing, you agree that your contribution is licensed under **CC BY 4.0**.

Questions? Open an issue: happy to help.
