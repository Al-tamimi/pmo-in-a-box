Some operational templates (e.g., several items under `03-build/` and `04-operate/`)

many organizations, and were adapted as neutral starting points. For all such files:
- All **third-party logos, trademarks, brand names, and identifying metadata** have
  been removed.
- Organization-, region-, and person-specific content has been replaced with neutral
