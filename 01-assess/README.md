# 01 · Assess

**Goal: get an honest baseline of where you stand today.**

You can't plan a credible improvement journey without knowing your starting point. This
stage uses the **P3M3 maturity model** to baseline capability and pinpoint gaps.

| File | What it's for |
|---|---|
| `P3M3-Maturity-Assessment.xlsx` | The P3M3 self-assessment. Covers **Portfolio, Programme, and Project** sub-models, each scored across **5 maturity levels × 7 perspectives**, with result and final-score sheets. |
| `Gap-Analysis-Template.docx` | Document the gap between your current state and your target state, perspective by perspective. |
| `Impact-Analysis.xlsx` | Assess the impact of the changes you're proposing, to help prioritize. |

### How to use this stage
1. Complete the **P3M3 self-assessment** honestly: score the level you *consistently*
   achieve, not your best day. Start with the sub-model that matters most to you.
2. Capture the findings in the **Gap Analysis** and agree a realistic target level.
3. Use the **Impact Analysis** to prioritize what to tackle first.

> See [`../docs/methodology.md`](../docs/methodology.md) for what the 5 levels and 7
> perspectives mean.

**Next:** [`02-design`](../02-design), design the PMO that closes the gaps.
